# Booking and Payment
- A non-refundable deposit of 25% of the total fee is required to secure the date and time of the performance, unless otherwise specified in writing. The booking is not confirmed until this deposit has been received. Payment of the deposit constitutes acceptance of these Terms and Conditions.
- The remaining 75% balance is due on the day of the performance or 48 hours prior to the event, unless otherwise specified in writing.
- Payments may be made via cheque/Interac e-Transfer/cash, payable to Soaring Eagles Lion Dance Arts and Sports Association.
# Cancellation and Refund Policy
- More than 30 days notice: If the client cancels the engagement more than one (1) month prior to the scheduled performance date, the client is entitled to a full refund of the deposit.
- Less than 30 days notice: If the Client cancels less than one (1) month prior to the event, the 25% deposit is forfeited and will be retained by Soaring Eagles Lion Dance Arts and Sports Association.
- In instances where a deposit was waived or a different amount was "otherwise specified" at the time of booking, the Client remains liable for a cancellation fee equivalent to 25% of the total invoice amount if cancelled with less than 30 days of notice.
# Weather Policy
- In the event of weather complications outside of our control, our team will notify the Client up to one (1) hour before the scheduled show if the performance needs to be cancelled.
- If a show is cancelled due to weather, a rescheduling will be offered. If the show cannot be rescheduled, the deposit will be refunded in full.
# Performance Logistics and Safety
- The client will provide a clear, flat, and dry surface for the performance.
- Flooring: For the safety of the performers, we are unable to perform on wet surfaces, gravel, and polished/slippery floors without prior assessment.
- Height clearance: If the performance includes elements of elevation, minimum ceiling heights must be respected to allow the performance to safely occur. Minimum ceiling heights include: all performances (10-12ft), high poles (20 ft), tables and benches (16ft).
- The client accepts that traditional lion dance performances include loud percussion (drums, gong, cymbals), and is responsible for notifying guests of the venue of high noise levels.
- To prevent injury to both performers and guests, the client is responsible for maintaining a safe distance between the performance area and the audience.
# Performance Delays
- The team will arrive 30 minutes or earlier prior to the scheduled performance time for setup.
- If the event runs late and the performance is delayed more than 30 minutes from the scheduled start time, an overtime fee of $100 per one (1) hour may apply.
# Contract Agreement
- This quotation becomes a binding contract upon receipt of the 25% deposit (unless otherwise specified), which officially secures the performance date and time. By paying the deposit, the Client agrees to the Terms and Conditions listed above.
